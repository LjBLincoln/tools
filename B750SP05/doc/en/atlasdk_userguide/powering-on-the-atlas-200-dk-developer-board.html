Powering on the Atlas 200 DK Developer Board

Precautions

Do not power off the Atlas 200 DK developer board while it is booting for the first time. Otherwise, the developer board may be damaged. To re-power on the developer board, wait at least 2s after it is powered off.

Procedure

  1. Plug in the power cable and connect the power module to the external power supply. Figure 1 shows the power interface on the Atlas 200 DK. Connect to the power supply, the Atlas 200 DK automatically boots.
