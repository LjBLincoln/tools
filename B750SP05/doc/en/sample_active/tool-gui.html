Tool GUI

Tool GUI

Open the Mind Studio tool. The tool GUI is displayed, as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1 Tool GUI

  • The menu bar on the top provides the functions of creating, editing, and deleting project and files, and compiling, running, and setting projects.
  • On the left are the project management, model management, and dataset management tabs. The project, model, or dataset details are displayed on the Projects Explorer window on the right. The Projects Explorer window shows the project details.
